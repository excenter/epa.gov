Climate Ready Estuaries

The Climate Ready EstuariesÂ program works with the National Estuary Programs and the coastal management community to:
  1. Assess climate change vulnerabilities,
  2. Develop and implement adaptation strategies, and
  3. Engage and educate stakeholders.

CRE shares NEP examples to help other coastal managers, and provides technical guidance and assistance about climate change adaptation.
