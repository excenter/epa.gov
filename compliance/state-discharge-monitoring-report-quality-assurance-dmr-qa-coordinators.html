State Discharge Monitoring Report - Quality Assurance (DMR-QA) Coordinators

For questions, your primary point of contact is your state DMR-QA coordinator. To find your state contact, select your state or territory from the map or list below. 

Reference your NPDES permit and telephone number on all correspondence.

You also may contact your regional DMR-QA coordinator.

States with DMR-QA Coordinators

Gray states have a laboratory accreditation program for wastewater deemed equivalent to the national DMR-QA program through a waiver agreement with EPA.
