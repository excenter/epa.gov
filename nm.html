  • U.S. - Mexico Border 2020 Program

    The U.S.-Mexico Border 2020 Program is a binational collaborative effort, between the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and Mexico’s Secretariat for Environment and Natural Resources, whose mission is to protect the environment and public health along the U.S.-Mexico border region.

EPA in New Mexico
