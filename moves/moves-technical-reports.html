MOVES Technical Reports

The technical reports for MOVES2014 incorporate the latest information included in EPA’s most current versions of MOVES: MOVES2014a and MOVES2014.  Technical reports for earlier versions of MOVES are included here because much of the technical information in the latest versions of MOVES builds off of information developed for previous versions.

You will need Adobe Reader to view some of the files on this page. See EPA’s About PDF page to learn more.
