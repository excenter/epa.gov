Significant Guidance Documents - Air

This page lists significant guidance documents issued by EPA's Office of Air and Radiation (OAR) or identified as a document for which OAR is the lead office. For every document, you may:

  • Comment on the document - Select "Comment" next to the relevant document to go to Regulations.gov, where you can view and comment on it. (Instructions for commenting.)
  • Learn more about the document and related EPA programs - Select "Learn More" to go to pages within the EPA Web site that give context to the document.

To browse the documents related to other environmental topics, select from the following list:

  • Chemical Safety and Pollution Prevention
  • Land and Emergency Management
  • Science
  • Water

You also may comment on how well we are complying with OMB's Good Guidance Bulletin.

Instructions for Commenting on Documents at Regulations.gov

When you follow the "Comment" links in the table above, you will be directed to the appropriate document details screen on Regulations.gov for the document you are interested in.

You may follow these steps to submit a comment:

  1. Click on the "Submit Comment" link found above the title of the document. (Note: If you do not see the icon next to "Add Comments," then the action is not currently accepting comments.)
  2. Fill out the form labeled "Submit a Comment" that appears.
  3. Review your information.
  4. When satisfied, click the "Submit" button.

Contact Us to ask a question, provide feedback, or report a problem.
