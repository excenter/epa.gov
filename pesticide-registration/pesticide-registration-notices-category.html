Pesticide Registration Notices by Category

The Office of Pesticide Programs Pesticide Registration (PR) Notices to inform pesticide registrants and other interested persons about important policies, procedures and regulatory decisions.They do not create new legally binding requirements.

This listing by category is intended to help you find the notices that are most relevant to your issue. PR notices can be listed in more than one category.

Note that this listing may not be comprehensive. That is, there could be notices that include content in a category, but that content was not identified when this categorization was developed. There also may be other categories that were not identified when we developed this list. If you find items in either category or have other questions about this list, please contact us

View PR Notices listed by the year they were issued.
