Water System Partnerships Meeting

Register Online Today

https://epa-watersystemspartnerships.eventbrite.com

Please join us on January 31, 2017 in Washington, D.C for a daylong meeting focused on water system partnerships, including promoting partnership activities and creating incentives for and reducing challenges to their development. This will be a working meeting with very limited presentations and a high emphasis on discussion and input from the participants. It is part of a collaborative vision to establish a framework to support partnership activities on a national and local level.

The working meeting will focus on three key theme areas: incentives and drivers to support water system partnership development, funding and finance options for partnerships, and outreach and education. Participants will actively engage and help drive the short term and long term actions needed for each of these areas. Participants will include federal agencies, state and local government, utilities, associations, private sector and non-profits.

For more information, contact: ogwdwprotectiontraining@epa.gov
