Meet our PESP Partners

The Pesticide Environmental Stewardship Program (PESP) is a voluntary membership program that promotes the adoption of innovative, alternative pest control practices such as Integrated Pest Management (IPM). PESP has three membership tiers: Bronze, Silver, and Gold. 

PESP Gold Members

Gold membership is reserved for PESP's outstanding environmental stewards. These members’ demonstrated commitment to pesticide risk reduction makes them models for other PESP members. Gold members may develop an IPM strategy, demonstrate significant and sustained progress in reducing pesticide risks, and independently measure the progress of their IPM implementation. 

PESP Silver Members

Silver membership is reserved for PESP's higher achieving environmental stewards. These members’ are well on their way in demonstrating a commitment to pesticide risk reduction and may serve as models for PESP Bronze-level members. Silver members may develop an IPM strategy and independently measure the progress of their IPM implementation.

PESP Bronze Members

Bronze membership is the starting point for new members entering the program. Bronze members affirm that environmental stewardship is an integral part of their approach to pest management and may independently measure the progress of their IPM implementation.
