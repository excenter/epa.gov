Sending to or calling a specific employee

  • Regular mail: please use the mailing address for a specific employee as listed in the EPA Employee Directory.
  • Package or delivery: please contact the employee and ask for the specific street address and any required delivery instructions.
  • Calling:  please use the phone number listed in the EPA Employee Directory.

Sending to a location without knowing the specific employee

Please use the addresses listed below. At headquarters, we use one address for all regular mail and specific building addresses for packages. In other locations, we use one address for both purposes.
