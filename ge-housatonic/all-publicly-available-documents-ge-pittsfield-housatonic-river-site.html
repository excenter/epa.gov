  • Tips on Locating Documents from the Table Listing
    • Search listings using keywords. An exact title is not necessary. The search will filter the table as you type.
    • You can search/filter all columns with the 'Search All Columns' search field which is located above the table. Additionally, you can narrow your search further by entering your keyword(s) to the column search field(s). Or you can search by column(s) only.
    • Click on the X button to clear all search fields. Or select the keyword(s) & press the delete key to clear column searches.
    • Sorting can be toggled by clicking on the desired column heading.
    • On mobile devices or narrow browser windows, you can toggle visibility of hidden row content by clicking on the green plus or red minus buttons.
    • This search database gets updated once per month, on approximately the 2nd Friday of each month.
