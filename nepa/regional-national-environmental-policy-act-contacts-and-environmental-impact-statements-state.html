Regional National Environmental Policy Act Contacts and Environmental Impact Statements by State

EPA has 10 regional offices, each of which is responsible for conducting environmental reviews under the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA). To get Regional NEPA contacts and a list of environmental impact statements (EISs) for your state/region, select your state or territory from the map or list below.
