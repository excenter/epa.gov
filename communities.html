Resources for Local Officials and Community Members

"Resources for Local Officials and Community Members" is a one-stop directory for local government officials and community members seeking to address today's complex environmental challenges at the local level.

Resources for Local Officials – EPA and State

The Local Government Environmental Assistance Network (LGEAN) Exit is a resource that helps local governments with compliance and other environmental management issues.

  • Ask LGEAN Exit or call 877-865-4326 with questions
  • Financing opportunities Exit
  • LGEAN City Exit

Resources for Local Officials – Cross Federal

The National Resource Network (NRN) Exit provides information and technical assistance to support economic growth and revitalization in communities.

  • Technical Assistance Clearinghouse Exit
  • Resource Library Exit
  • 311 for Cities Exit

Resources for Community Members

The Environmental Health Resources for Community Members site provides tools and information to help local leaders protect public health by understanding and addressing environmental conditions.
