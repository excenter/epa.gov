Featured Publication

Title: A Citizen’s Guide to Radon: The Guide to Protecting Yourself and Your Family from Radon
Publication: 402-K-12-002B
Published: 2016

Radon is a naturally occurring radioactive gas that can cause lung cancer.  You can’t see or smell radon. Testing is the only way to know your level of exposure.  Radon can have a big impact on indoor air quality. Find out how to test for radon in your home.
