Fluorinated Gas Partnership Programs

The Fluorinated Gas Partnership Programs were launched as a joint effort by EPA and industry groups to reduce the amount of fluorinated gases (F-Gas) emitted from a variety of industrial processes.Â  The programs promote the development and adoption of cost-effective F-Gas emission reduction opportunities.
