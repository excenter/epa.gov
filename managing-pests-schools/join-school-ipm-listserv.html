Join the School IPM Listserv

You’re Invited to Join the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency’s School IPM Listserv!

By subscribing, you will receive timely and helpful information on school IPM about once every two weeks.  Included will be invitations to upcoming webinars, highlights from and links to IPM-related newsletters, updates on EPA’s school IPM activities, and other announcements.

EPA recommends that all schools have an Integrated Pest Management (IPM) program to reduce the risks to students and staff from pests and pesticides. The School IPM Listserv is our newest way to assist and motivate those in the school community to start, grow, and sustain their IPM programs.

Simply provide us with some basic information, including your email address, and you’re all set. Rest assured that the information you provide in signing up will be kept private. If you have questions regarding the listserv, you can email us or call us (toll-free) at 844-EPA-SIPM.

Please take a moment to join now!
