On March 18, EPA issued a modification of the EPA time-critical action memo for the cleanup at Camp Minden.

Action Memorandum Time-Critical Removal for M6 and CBI – Amended (3/18/2015) (PDF) (23  pp, 1 MB, About PDF)

The amended action memo allows alternative technologies to be considered in disposing of the materials abandoned by Explo and left deteriorating at the site. The amendment clears the way for the Louisiana Military Department to review quotes for alternative methods and select a company to dispose of the M6 and CBI.

The original Administrative Record has been amended to include these documents in support of the amended action memorandum. View the original Administrative Record.

This page contains the Amended Administrative Record documents and a link to the Original Administrative Record for the Camp Minden site in Minden, Louisiana.
