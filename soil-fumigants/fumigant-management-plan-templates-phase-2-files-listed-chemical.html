Fumigant Management Plan Templates - Phase 2 Files Listed by Chemical


Related Information

Templates for Phase 1 labels

EPA created templates in PDF and Word formats for the management plan for each soil fumigant. 

  • The PDF templates are for users to print and fill out by hand.
  • The Word templates are for users to fill out on the computer.
The agency also created samples for each chemical of filled-out:
  • Fumigant management plans (FMP).
  • Post-Application Summary reports.
  • Related forms.

The latter two items are included in the fumigant management plan sample.

Since label requirements vary based on the soil fumigant, please use the appropriate chemical-specific templates. Note that phase 2 labels can be identified by the presence of a "Terms used in this labeling" section.
