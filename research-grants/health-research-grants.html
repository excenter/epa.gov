Health Research Grants

EPA provides grants and funding to the nation's leading researchers to improve the scientific basis for decisions on health and environmental issues.

Learn more about health research grants in the following areas:

  • Centers of Excellence on Environmental Health Disparities Research
  • Children’s Environmental Health and Disease Prevention Research Centers
  • Healthy Schools Research Grants
  • Tribal Environmental Health Research

  • Using a Total Environment Framework (Built, Natural, Social Environments) to Assess Life-long Health Effects of Chemical Exposures
    Open: Dec. 2, 2016 - Close: March 2, 2017
