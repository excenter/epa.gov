Natural Gas STAR Outreach and Events

Facilitating information exchange on cost-effective methane emissions reduction technologies and practices is the core mission of the Natural Gas STAR Program. The Natural Gas STAR Program hosts a series of Technology Transfer Workshops and the Annual Implementation Workshop to further this goal.

Recent Event

Technology Transfer Workshop - Pipeline Blowdowns in Transmission and Distribution
Houston, Texas - November 10, 2016
EPA’s Natural Gas STAR and Methane Challenge Programs hosted this one-day workshop to provide information and insights for natural gas companies and other stakeholders interested in achieving and promoting methane emission reductions from pipeline blowdowns. View the workshop agenda and presentations.

Methane Challenge Program Best Management Practice Webinars

EPA hosted a series of webinars to provide details about the proposed Natural Gas STAR Methane Challenge Program (“Methane Challenge”). The webinars provided an overview of the proposed Methane Challenge and the proposed Best Management Practices (BMPs) to reduce methane emissions from key recommended sources. Each webinar included a question and answer session and participants were encouraged to provide feedback. Three webinars were conducted for the following sectors:

Natural Gas Distribution Sector

Tuesday, July 28, 2015, 1:00 to 3:00 PM EDT
View the webinar presentation

Natural Gas Transmission and Underground Storage Sector

Wednesday, July 29, 2015, 1:00 to 3:00 PM EDT
View the webinar presentation
