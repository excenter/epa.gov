The U.S. EPA’s voluntary oil and gas methane programs—including Natural Gas STAR, Natural Gas STAR International, and Methane Challenge—are designed to promote the implementation of cost-effective technologies and practices to reduce methane emissions. The partnerships include oil and natural gas companies operating both domestically in the U.S. and abroad.
